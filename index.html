Can I watch the game if I am outside the U.S.?

Absolutely! If you're traveling or living abroad, you can still catch all the action by using a VPN (Virtual Private Network). A VPN allows you to connect to a server in the U.S., making it appear as though you're accessing content from home.

Steps to Use a VPN:

  1. Choose a reputable VPN service (like ExpressVPN or NordVPN).
  1. Install and set up the VPN on your device.
  1. Connect to a U.S.-based server.
  1. Access your streaming service as usual.

This way, you won’t miss any crucial plays or highlights while enjoying your travels.
